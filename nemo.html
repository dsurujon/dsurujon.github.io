About the Collection

Meet my cat Nemo . He has brought so much joy and inspiration into my life. I hope to capture his radiance, his quirks, and the unique bond we share through this collection.

The Boy's Journey:

  • The boy: The boy, the legend himself.
  • The roll: It took some time but he came to love belly rubs.
  • Greenies: This is the face he makes when I shake the greenies bin.
  • The snooze: A quick sketch of Nemo sleeping next to me.
  • The owl: We're getting a little abstract, a little weird here.
  • Greenies - intensified: The intensity of the stare I get when the man demands his treats
  • The blob: I think this one is on a shipping box.
  • The couch: The plants are (from left to right) pineapple, calathea, monstera. I don't think they ever overlapped in time like this. And the couch isn't purple. You're getting a distorted version of reality.
  • The light: I miss you already buddy.
